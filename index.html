My Story

My Story

I am a creative at heart with a love for technology.

Throughout my life, I have had an affinity for creation. As a child, I found any excuse I could to retreat into my mind and make my ideas come to life. My addiction and love for creation grew throughout high school where I pursued Fine Art and took every opportunity to try something new. When others were painting or drawing, I was building wire structures or sculpting in clay and wax.

When I left high school, I thought I wanted to be a fashion designer but I had doubts; which led me to take a few years to figure out what I wanted to do. In those years, I travelled, worked and learned some harsh lessons about myself and how the world works.

In my years off, I found a love for technology and saw the potential in digital design. Initially, I wanted to go into marketing, but I soon realized that I needed to take a more creative, hands-on approach to the industry.

Although it has been a challenge to keep up with this course, having no background in design, I have enjoyed it immensely and I have learned more than I could have ever imagined. That being said I still have a lot of knowledge to gain and I cannot wait to learn more in the industry.

My Future

My Future

My aim is to go into the industry as soon as possible, so that I can expand on the knowledge I have gained this year while it is all still fresh in my mind. I would like to work as a front-end developer with a design team so that I can learn from them while growing my web development skills.

I also hope to go back to Friends of Design to take the part-time Print course in the near future so that I can reach the level that I want to be at with my UI design.

In the long term I would like to delve deeper into creative web design, making websites into a work of art while still focussing on giving the user what they need. I am excited to be a part of the future of Web Development, the possibilities are endless and I so look forward to exploring as many of them as I can.
